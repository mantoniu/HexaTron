Step into the digital arena of HexaTron, a thrilling strategy game inspired by the classic light-cycle battles of Tron but with a hexagonal twist. Unlike the traditional grid-based gameplay, HexaTron takes place on a hexagonal grid that creates more movement possibilities and strategic depth.

Whether you're looking for casual fun with friends or competitive ranked matches to test your skills, HexaTron offers multiple ways to enjoy the hexagonal battlefield. Master the unique movement patterns, develop advanced strategies, and claim your place among the elite HexaTron players.

No matter which game mode you choose, each match is played in 3 rounds.
Your objective is simple: be the last one standing at each round and win more rounds than your opponent.
The rules are simple: to win a round, avoid falling out of the arena, crashing into your opponent’s trail, or running into your own.
Master the arena, outsmart your opponent, and claim victory!

Here you have a presentation of the game’s control system.
Player 1, is the main character you will control in every game mode. Player 2, are controls only used during local game by the other player.

On the diagram, each colored hexagon represents a player’s trail.
You control your ship using four keys, but the controls rotate with your ship.
This means the keys always move you based on your ship’s current orientation on the hexagonal grid.
Tips: if the default keys don’t feel quite right, you can fully customize them in the settings menu to match your preferred style of play.
