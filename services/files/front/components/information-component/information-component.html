Step into the digital arena of HexaTron, a game inspired by the classic light-cycle battles of Tron but with a hexagonal twist.

You can choose between four different ways to play:

Challenge your friends on the same device Practice against computer opponent Test your skills against players worldwide Compete against online friends

In any game mode, each match is played in 3 rounds where your goal is to outlast your opponent by avoiding crashes, staying in the arena, and winning more rounds to claim victory.

Here you have a presentation of the game’s control system.

Player 1, is the main character you will control in every game mode. Player 2, are controls only used during local game by the other player.

Each colored hexagon on the diagram represents a player’s trail, and since controls rotate with your ship’s orientation on the hex grid, you can customize the keys in the settings to suit your play style.

You will control your ship using a joystick in the bottom left corner. If you're playing locally, a second joystick will be available for the second player.
